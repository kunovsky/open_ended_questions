Open Ended Questions
THE HEART & SOUL
OF YOUR PARTNER
Want to know the heart and soul of your partner? The answers to these questions reveal who he or she really is, beyond the surface of every day routine.

When you are both relaxed and content, choose one of these to discuss, and follow the threads of conversation until you have each exhausted the topic. Be curious and interested if you are the listener, and ask more questions as you think of them, if your partner is open to it.
